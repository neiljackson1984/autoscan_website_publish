Drafting:


At Autoscan, Inc. we take your ideas, concepts, and rough sketches, and convert them into dimensionally accurate CAD files. Using our drafting services will ensure that your company will complete projects in a timely manner and enable your team to concentrate on more important tasks. We have a history of excellence and our drafters understand that the quality of their work reflects on both themselves and your company. We offer drafting as well as basic design services.

For short or long-term needs, Autoscan, Inc. provides highly trained CAD Specialists experienced with CAD software and with the ability to communicate effectively with project managers. Our technicians are experts in all releases of AutoCAD, Microstation, Land Development Desktop/Softdesk, Autoturn, SolidWorks, and 3D StudioMax. Each technician brings a wealth of experience gained from working on various disciplines and jobsites, which enables them to be adaptive and innovative. With a large support staff, our employees are able to call in extra help when needed, or send work back to the office for processing. In addition to technical drafting, we offer the following:

* Project Management, Set-up/Creation of Project Standards
* Terrain Modeling with Land Development software
* Vehicle/Aircraft Path simulation with Autoturn
