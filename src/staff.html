Staff:



Autoscan, Inc. Executive Principals

Jeff McNabb, Co-Founder and President

Jeff McNabb was born and raised in Seattle. He graduated from the University of Washington where he received his B.S. in Mechanical Engineering. After co-founding Autoscan, Inc. Jeff returned to school, receiving a Masters in Finance from Seattle University. Jeff now resides with his wife Elaine and two children in Seattle.

Bob Boulware, Co-Founder and Vice President

Bob Boulware was raised in the Seattle area and graduated from Swarthmore College where he received his B.S in Engineering. He continued his education at U.C. Berkeley receiving a M.S. in Mechanical Engineering. On returning to Seattle Bob and Jeff established Autoscan, Inc. He lives on Bainbridge Island with his wife Barbara and three children.

Peter Wong, Senior Partner

Peter Wong grew up in the Puget Sound area. He attended the University of Washington where he met and became friends with Jeff, and received a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering. A year after Autoscan, Inc.’s inception, Peter was asked to join the company and soon became Senior Partner. Peter lives in Seattle.

Tom Evans, Associate (Colorado branch Manager)

Tom Evans grew up in Parker, Colorado. He attended the University of Colorado where he received a B.Envd in Architecture. After eight years of working for various architecture firms in Colorado, Tom headed out to the Pacific Northwest. There he met Jeff on the soccer field. They became friends, and in 2005 Tom was asked to join the company. In 2008 Tom returned to Colorado to open a branch office.
