About:



Autoscan, Inc.

With over 14 years of experience Autoscan, Inc offers on-site and off-site services within the Puget Sound area to meet both your immediate and long-term needs. We specialize in:

* Architectural and Engineering drafting
* Network design, installation and support
* Website design and development


We are able to provide support services for architectural, aviation, civil, structural, electrical, and mechanical drafting in all releases of AutoCAD, Microstation, Land Development Desktop, and Solidworks. In addition, we offer architectural as-built services, raster to vector conversion of architectural and engineering drawings, full size color and monochrome plotting, and 3-D rendering and animation.

We understand that it is important to work in accordance with your standards and procedures in order to create a seamless integration between our company and your in-house staff. At Autoscan, Inc. we believe that customer satisfaction, quality of work, and rapid turnaround time are of utmost importance. We are confident that we can meet and exceed the level of service you expect and require.
